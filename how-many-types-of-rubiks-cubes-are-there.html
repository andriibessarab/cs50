How many types of Rubik's Cubes are there?

There are lots of different variations of Rubik's Cubes with up to 33 layers. It sounds weird, but they are not always cubes. Sometimes they have very strange forms. Some of them people use on competitions. While others they are solving just for fun. Or to challenge themselves.You can read more about Rubik's Cube Competitions here.

Interesting Fact: Some people even build huge arts with hundreds or even thousands of Rubik's Cubes.
